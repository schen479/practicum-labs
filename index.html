About Practicum Labs

Practicum Labs was founded to address a vital need: students want to get into research but often lack the support or connections to succeed.
Our goal is to empower every ambitious learner with the guidance, skills, and mentorship needed to unlock their potential and turn ideas into impact.
Who we reach: Students from all backgrounds, including those with disabilities, women in STEM, neurodivergent students, K-12 and college levels, from all locations/genders.
